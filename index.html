What is interesting about Jakarta?


Before we talk quite deep about what's in Jakarta, let me tell you WHAT is Jakarta.

DKI Jakarta is a Capital City of Indonesia and one of the biggest city is Indonesia. It is the only city in Indonesia that has the status of province. Jakarta is located on the northwest coast of Java. Previously it was known by several names including Sunda Kelapa, Jayakarta, and Batavia. In the international world Jakarta also has the nickname J-Town, or more popular The Big Durian because it is considered a city comparable to New York City (Big Apple) in Indonesia.

And here are few general facts about Jakarta.


Economic

Jakarta has a lot of culture, because in addition to being the capital, Jakarta is also the economic center of the Indonesian state, therefore, the city of Jakarta is a place where residents who live outside of Jakarta come here to look for jobs with higher salaries, with their current expectations returned to their hometown, they brought a lot of money for their families


Population

The population of DKI Jakarta in 2019 is projected to reach 10.5 million. This figure increased by 0.73% from the target population in 2018, which amounted to 10.4 million people. The total female population in Jakarta is 5.3 million while the male population is 5.2 million. By age, the highest contributor to the total population of DKI Jakarta is in the productive age. The total population of DKI Jakarta aged 15-64 years is recorded at 7.6 million. The highest number of population is in the age range of 25-29 years of 944.3 thousand inhabitants, 35-39 years of 931.7 thousand inhabitants, and 30-34 years of 926.2 thousand inhabitants.
